THE FIX:

  Hook FUN_140360B30 at RVA 0x360B30 with signature
    void __fastcall(this, uint scanCode)

  When IsModalUiActive() returns true, drop the call (early return).
  Otherwise pass through to original. This bypasses the binding tree
  walk entirely while chat or our menu is open — no hotkey fires.

  We deliberately do NOT toggle (this+0xd0) because Kenshi may already
  use that flag for its own pause/dialog states; competing for it
  could fight Kenshi's UI logic.

  The existing keyDownEvent (0x360680) hook stays — it gates a SEPARATE
  OIS::KeyListener (the one tracking held-keys at this+0x08). Same for
  the MyGUI inject-key hooks. Layered defense; minimal cost.
